XAU/USD Scalping — Pre-Trade Checklist

Run top to bottom. If any NO-GO gate trips, you don't click. No trade is a position.

1 Calendar & Event Risk

  • High-impact USD release in next 30–60 min? (CPI, PCE, NFP, FOMC, Fed speakers)
  • If yes → stand aside or plan the spike: wider stop, smaller size, no tight stops.
  • Gold spread can jump from ~20–40¢ to several $ at release. Assume slippage.
  • Check: ForexFactory / broker calendar before every session.

2 Session & Liquidity

  • Trading the London–NY overlap (~08:00–11:00 ET)? Tightest spreads, cleanest moves.
  • Asian session = chop. Thin edge — size down or skip.
  • Spread normal right now? If abnormally wide → no entry.
  • Enough ATR / range to clear spread + a sane R:R? If not, skip.

3 Macro Confluence (live)

  • DXY direction — gold moves inverse. Don't scalp long into a ripping dollar.
  • US10Y / real yields rising → gold headwind. Falling → tailwind.
  • Bias for the day set by yields+dollar, not by one CPI headline.
  • Trade with the macro tape, not against it.

4 Level & Trigger

  • Price at a level? Prior-day H/L, overnight range, opening range, VWAP, round #s (4000 / 4050).
  • Never scalp the middle of the range.
  • Mechanical trigger on 1m/5m: break-and-retest, rejection wick, momentum confirm.
  • Stop + target defined before entry. Know your invalidation.
GO
Calendar clear · overlap session · normal spread · macro confluence · price at level · trigger fired · stop set
WAIT
At a level but no trigger yet · spread slightly wide · range too tight → let it come to you
NO-GO
High-impact release imminent · fighting DXY/yields · mid-range · spread blown out · no defined stop
